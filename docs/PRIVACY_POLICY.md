# Privacy Policy for ClearNowX

**Last Updated: December 13, 2024**

## 1. Introduction

ClearNowX ("App," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly
- **Subscription Information**: When you purchase a subscription, we collect payment information processed through Apple App Store (we do not store payment card details)
- **App Preferences**: Preferences and settings you configure within the App
- **Support Communications**: If you contact us for support, we may collect the information you provide

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (via Apple's App Tracking Transparency)
- **Usage Analytics**: Which features you use, scanning frequency, and app interaction patterns
- **Performance Data**: App crashes, performance metrics, and error logs for improvement purposes

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- **File Contents**: We do NOT access, scan, or view the actual contents of your files
- **Personal Data**: We do NOT collect your name, email, phone number, or other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it for support
- **Location Data**: We do NOT access your location information
- **Contacts/Calendars**: We do NOT access your contacts, calendars, or other personal data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the App and our services
- Process subscription payments and manage your account
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
- Fix bugs and optimize app performance
- Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:
- **Apple**: For payment processing through App Store subscription management
- **Service Providers**: Third-party analytics providers (e.g., Firebase, Sentry) that assist in app improvement
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

## 5. Data Retention

- **Subscription Data**: Retained as long as your subscription is active
- **Analytics Data**: Typically retained for 90 days before anonymization
- **Support Data**: Retained for up to 1 year to resolve issues
- **Payment Information**: Not stored by us; managed by Apple's payment system

## 6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure API endpoints with authentication
- Regular security audits
- No storage of sensitive payment information on our servers

However, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
- You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- You can request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
- You can opt out of analytics collection in app settings

### App Store Privacy
- You can manage privacy settings on your Apple device
- You can reset your Advertising ID in Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising
- You can disable App Tracking Transparency permissions for ClearNowX

## 8. Children's Privacy

ClearNowX is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child without parental consent, we will delete that information promptly.

## 9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold
- Delete personal information collected from you
- Opt-out of the "sale" of personal information (we do not sell data)
