Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

ClearNowX ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our privacy practices for the ClearNowX mobile application and explains what information we collect, use, and disclose about you.

2. Information We Collect

ClearNowX is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal information:

  • Device Storage Analysis: Our app analyzes your device's storage locally on your device. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store data about your files, photos, videos, or personal documents.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymized usage data (e.g., feature usage, app performance metrics) to improve our service.
  • Subscription Information: Payment information is processed through Apple App Store and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment details directly.
  • Support Communications: When you contact support, we collect your email and message content to respond to your inquiry.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for:

  • Providing and improving the ClearNowX app
  • Responding to your customer support inquiries
  • Understanding how you use our app to enhance features
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

For complete transparency, ClearNowX does NOT:

  • Access or store your personal files, photos, videos, or documents
  • Collect location data
  • Collect contacts or personal information
  • Track user behavior across apps
  • Share data with third parties for marketing
  • Store payment information (handled by Apple/RevenueCat)

5. Third-Party Services

ClearNowX uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: Handles payments and subscriptions
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription delivery and entitlements
  • Mixpanel: Anonymized analytics to improve app performance

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

6. Data Storage and Security

All storage analysis performed by ClearNowX happens locally on your device. No data is uploaded to our servers. Your device maintains complete control over all personal information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your account information
  • Opt-out of analytics collection
  • Request information about what data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data

8. Compliance with Laws

ClearNowX complies with applicable privacy laws including CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) and GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation). If you are located in California or the EU, you have specific rights under these regulations.

9. Children's Privacy

ClearNowX is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete such information promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date above.
